Favorite Sites

The above window is your Max Favorites and can be accessed from the Max menu. This holds information on sites you specify so they can be easily accessed at any time.

Adding, and Opening Favorites

To add sites to your favorites, select a Folder and click on New Site under the action menu, or by right clicking. The site will be created under the folder you selected. You can do the same and use the New Folder button to make another Folder.

To edit a site, select the site and then click on Edit Site under the action menu. You will see the window below.

The first thing to enter in is Comments. Comments can be anything it's just for your reference. The next option is whether the site is a Double Site, Primary Site, or Secondary Site. By clicking Double Site you will be allowed to enter two locations that will load in one client window. By clicking Primary Site, you will only be able to enter one site that will load in a single client window. By clicking Secondary Site, you will only be able to enter one site, and it will load a double client window with your secondary URL in the second half of the window. You open your favorite site by first selecting the site, and then clicking on Open Site under the Action menu.

Deleting, and Renaming Favorites

To Delete a site or folder, select the item you whish to delete, and click on Delete under the action menu; this removed any information on the drive as well where the sites are located under the MaxFTP favorites folder in the installation folder

To Rename a site or folder, select the item you whish to rename, and either click it again (not a double click), or click on Rename under the action menu. You will then be able to enter text inside the items outline box, as shown below.
